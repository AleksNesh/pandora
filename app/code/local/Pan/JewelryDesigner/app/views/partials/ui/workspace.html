How to get started:

  1. Choose a Bracelet and then select the size
  2. Choose Charms with meaning or by style
  3. Choose Clips for balance on bracelets with threaded areas
  4. Review Your Design to save or purchase. You will have the option to edit / remove items in your Shopping Bag

Please note: Our bracelet builder features a 19cm bracelet. The number of charms that will fit on your bracelet will depend on actual bracelet size and selected charms width. Leather bracelets are intended for a maximum of 7-9 charms. Items shown represent the current line of PANDORA bracelets and charms.
